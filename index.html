It's Uber for Market Swipes
We use texts to match people who want to sell swipes with people who want swiped in.

How Does It Work?

  1. Seller sets her asking price on the SwipeMe website.
  2. Seller "checks in" by sending a text to SwipeMe when she goes to Market.
  3. Buyer sends a text to SwipeMe when he wants swiped in.
  4. SwipeMe matches him with the seller in Market with the lowest asking price.
  5. If the buyer accepts the price, then the seller gets a text telling her to find the buyer and swipe him in.
  6. The buyer pays in cash.
